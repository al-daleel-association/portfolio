Introduction

Founded in 2018, Al Daleel Association has become a trusted name in community empowerment and sustainable development in Baalback Hermel. We are dedicated to promoting social justice, gender equality, and the protection of children and marginalized groups through comprehensive programs and advocacy.

About Us

Al Daleel Association is committed to enhancing the quality of life for vulnerable populations by addressing their immediate needs and providing long-term support. Our focus areas include women's empowerment, children's welfare, and community development. We strive to create lasting positive change through innovative solutions and dedicated service.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a more equitable society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive regardless of their background. We envision a future where marginalized communities are fully integrated into the social fabric, with access to resources, opportunities, and support to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and uplift disadvantaged individuals and communities by providing support, advocacy, and resources. We are dedicated to promoting social inclusion, protecting human rights, and fostering sustainable development to create a better future for all.

Current Endeavors

Women

Al Daleel Association is dedicated to empowering marginalized groups of women and children. Our efforts encompass enhancing women's economic, social, and cultural empowerment, advocating for gender equality, and defending women's rights. We aim to support women in both personal and professional growth, urging their active participation in the community's decision-making processes. Our initiatives include comprehensive programs for the overall development of women, fostering solidarity, and improving communication within the community.

Children

Al Daleel Association is dedicated to protecting children and providing a friendly and safe environment to foster their growth and development. Our initiatives focus on ensuring access to quality education, offering support for their emotional and psychological well-being, and creating programs that stimulate their talents and interests. We are committed to safeguarding children's rights and ensuring they have the resources and opportunities needed to thrive and reach their full potential.

Objectifs

Empowering women economically, socially and culturally, fostering their active engagement in diverse community roles and creating a supportive environment for personal and professional growth.

Supporting marginalized and vulnerable communities, the focus is on ensuring their protection, providing equal access to opportunities, and fostering gender equality.

Ensuring the protection of children and providing a secure environment, our objective is to enhance their mental and physical well-being, guaranteeing equitable access to rights for all children, regardless of differences.

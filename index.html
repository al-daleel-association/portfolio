Welcome to Al Daleel Association

Founded in 2018, Al Daleel Association has become a trusted name in community empowerment and sustainable development in Baalback Hermel. We are dedicated to promoting social justice, gender equality, and human rights. Our work is focused on providing resources, support, and education to marginalized communities across the region.

Founded in 2018 in Lebanon and registered as a non-profit with the Ministry of Interior (Number 1959), Al Daleel is a local civil organization. Our commitment lies in serving the community, with a particular focus on empowering marginalized groups. We are dedicated to economically and socially empowering women, encouraging their active participation in society. Moreover, our efforts extend to supporting children in their education, fostering their skills, and ensuring their protection and well-being. Al Daleel Association aspires to create a brighter future for the local community through these initiatives.

Al Daleel Association envisions a future where women and children, regardless of background, have equal access to opportunities and resources. We aspire to lead in creating a society characterized by the empowerment of women, the well-being of children, and sustainable development. Through our unwavering commitment to fostering positive change, we aim to be a catalyst for a more just, inclusive, and prosperous community, one that is not only advanced but also more human and equitable.

At Al Daleel Association, our mission is to foster partnerships and collaboration with diverse stakeholders engaged in addressing the social and economic gaps for women and children. Through our work, we aim to contribute to the creation of a more developed and equitable society.

Empowering women economically, socially and culturally, fostering their active engagement in diverse community roles and creating a supportive environment for personal and professional growth.

Supporting marginalized and vulnerable communities, the focus is on ensuring their protection, providing equal access to opportunities, and fostering gender equality.

Ensuring the protection of children and providing a secure environment, our objective is to enhance their mental and physical well-being, guaranteeing equitable access to rights for all children, regardless of differences.
